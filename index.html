I am a PhD student at UCLA in the department of Information Studies where I am advised by Dr. Safiya Noble

My research is focused on the social aspects of mathematical knowledge, particularly the mathematical and computational methods behind AI, algorithms, and information systems. I am interested in how mathematical methods and formalizations affect the algorithmic technologies we use everyday and how we can create just liberatory technologies.

My interests stretch across critical scholarship on technology, mathematics research, and computational methods

Research Interests
Critical AI Studies, Critical Data Studies, ML Fairness and Ethics, Race and Digital Justice, Science and Technology Studies, Black Computational Thought

Math Research Interests
Mathematics of Machine Learning, Fairness in Nonnegative Matrix Factorization, Abstract Algebra, Algebraic Geometry, Mathematical Logic/Model Theory
